Services Provided
Tired of battling unruly leaves, stubborn yard waste, or grimy outdoor surfaces around your Tulsa home or business?

At ECO Revive Project, we understand that maintaining a pristine outdoor space can be a time-consuming and often back-breaking task.

That's where we come in.

As your dedicated outdoor cleanup specialists right here in Tulsa, Oklahoma, we offer a comprehensive suite of services designed to reclaim your weekends and enhance the beauty and functionality of your property.

From seasonal leaf removal and thorough gutter cleaning to power washing revitalizing patios and efficient storm debris cleanup, ECO Revive Project is committed to providing reliable, efficient, and professional outdoor maintenance solutions tailored to your specific needs.

Let us handle the dirty work, so you can relax and enjoy your outdoor oasis.

Types of Services

Yard Waste Removal: This is a core service, involving the collection and disposal of leaves, branches, grass clippings, and other organic debris generated from yard work.

General Property Cleanup: This could include removing litter, debris, fallen fruit, pinecones, and other unwanted items from lawns, gardens, and driveways.

Leaf Removal: Often a seasonal service, this focuses specifically on the efficient removal of fallen leaves, which can be time-consuming for homeowners.

Gutter Cleaning: Clearing out leaves, twigs, and other blockages from rain gutters and downspouts to ensure proper water drainage and prevent damage.

Minor Landscaping Cleanup: This might involve tasks like weeding garden beds, trimming overgrown shrubs or hedges (within limits, not full-scale landscaping), and tidying up mulch or rock areas.

Storm Debris Cleanup: After a storm, they can remove fallen branches, limbs, and other debris to make the property safe and presentable.

Construction Site Cleanup (Exterior): For smaller projects or post-construction, they can remove leftover materials, packaging, and general debris from the outdoor areas.

Estate Cleanup: Assisting with the often overwhelming task of cleaning up the outdoor spaces of a deceased person's property.

Power Washing: Cleaning patios, decks, driveways, sidewalks, and even the exterior of homes to remove dirt, grime, mold, and mildew.

Fence Line Clearing: Removing weeds, vines, and overgrowth along fence lines.

Seasonal Cleanup Packages: Offering bundled services for spring and fall cleanup to prepare properties for the changing seasons.

Hauling Services: Transporting larger items or significant amounts of debris off-site for proper disposal or recycling.

Equipment

For Waste and Debris Removal:

Leaf Blowers: To gather leaves, grass clippings, and other light debris into manageable piles. They might have handheld or backpack models for different property sizes.

Rakes: Various types, including leaf rakes (plastic or metal), garden rakes, and thatching rakes, for manual collection of debris.

Shovels and Spades: For scooping up heavier debris, mulch, or soil. Different sizes and types (round point, square) might be necessary.

Brooms: Stiff-bristled brooms for sweeping sidewalks, driveways, and patios.

Litter Pickers/Grabbers: For safely and easily collecting scattered trash and debris without bending over.

Wheelbarrows or Yard Carts: To transport collected waste and materials around the property.

Heavy-Duty Trash Bags or Bins: For containing and hauling away the collected debris. They might provide their own reusable bags or bins.

Tarps: To gather large amounts of leaves or debris for easier transport.

For Gutter Cleaning:

Ladders: Of various heights to safely access gutters.

Gutter Cleaning Tools: Scoops, brushes, and specialized attachments for removing debris from gutters.

Hoses with Attachments: To flush out remaining debris.

Gutter Vacuum Systems: For a more efficient and sometimes safer way to remove gutter debris from the ground.

For Minor Landscaping Cleanup:

String Trimmers (Weed Whackers): For trimming grass and weeds along edges, fences, and around obstacles.

Hedge Trimmers: For shaping and maintaining hedges and shrubs. They might have different sizes and power types (electric, battery, gas).

Pruning Shears and Loppers: For trimming smaller branches and overgrown shrubs.

Hand Cultivators and Weeders: For removing weeds from garden beds.

Edgers: For creating clean and defined edges along sidewalks and driveways.

For Power Washing Services:

Power Washer: Gas-powered or electric units with varying pressure capabilities depending on the surfaces being cleaned.

Various Nozzles and Attachments: For different cleaning tasks, such as wide sprays for general cleaning and focused jets for stubborn dirt.

Hoses and Extension Wands: To reach different areas.

Cleaning Solutions: Biodegradable and appropriate for outdoor surfaces.

Transportation and Safety:

Truck or Van: To transport equipment and crew to job sites. Larger businesses might have trailers for hauling more equipment.

Fuel and Maintenance Supplies: For gas-powered equipment.

Safety Gear: Including gloves, safety glasses, hearing protection, and appropriate work attire for their employees.
